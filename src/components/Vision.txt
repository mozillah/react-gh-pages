Vision
An internationally recognized testing and evaluation organization that enables it to produce 
educated and competitive market individuals. 

Mission
To develop and promote educational and professional testing and assessment standards.

Objectives
• Conducting effective, transparent and international quality tests to assess candidates' 
qualifications, abilities, training and employment. 
• Conducting research on education, technical and assessment programs to identify 
existing educational and operational gaps in programs.
• Generating and disseminating information and research on education and technology 
development standards.
• Capacity building of individuals and educational institutions through training and 
assessment.
